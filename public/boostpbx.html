Boost PBX< Return to Services

Boost PBX gives your campaign office(s) an enterprise-level telephone system that is fully hosted and managed at an extremely affordable cost. Our full featured PBX system includes the following features:

  • Toll-free and/or local phone number for inbound calls
  • IVR (Phone Tree / Menu)
  • Call conference rooms
  • Call queues
  • Unlimited extensions
  • Voicemail (with email notifications of new messages)
  • Call forwarding
  • Music on hold
  • Local & long distance phone service

With BoostPBX you can have a big business phone system, without the big price tag and cost associated with installing phone lines.  Our VoIP based solution allows your staffers to connect to the phone system from anywhere an Internet connection is available.
