Boost CRM< Return to Services

There is nothing more critical to any business than having up-to-date information that is easily accessible at the time it is needed.

Political Boost starts with information that your campaign already has, and builds a custom database to store the existing information.  Through our other modules, your campaign is able to expand the breadth of information, for each contact, by tracking every interaction a constituent has with your campaign. 

With Boost CRM, this information is placed at the fingertips of campaign staffers using a simple interface for querying the database. 

Additionally, Boost CRM is integrated with Boost PBX and the Predictive Dialer, so that every inbound & outbound phone call to/from your campaign creates a ‘screen-pop’ to the campaign staffer which displays useful information & history (from the database) relating to the person with which they are speaking.
