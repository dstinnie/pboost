Caller Questions & Answers


Who / What is Committee for Political Progress?
We are a small organization with a particular interest in keeping people involved with local & national politics.  The 2008 Presidential election was a great start for increased political involvement and our mission is to keep that momentum going.  Our primary goals are to encourage people to be informed about political issues and candidates.  Voting for the Presidency is a great start, but the issues that most directly affect you as a constituent revolves around your locally elected officials.  So, we are a grassroots organization with a mission of driving constituents to be informed about local politics and candidates, and get them to the polls to vote in municipal, county, and statewide elections.

Where are you located?
We are based in Washington, D.C. but have small offices around the country.

Where are you calling from?
For security reasons, I can not disclose my specific location.

What Party are you affiliated with?
We are a non-partisan organization only concerned with voter turnout and increased involvement with politics.
